Design Improvement Project

My Design Improvement

For this project, I chose a design I thought I could improve on. Here are the problems I identified with the initial design:

  • Background color: Bright blue is to bright and doesn't complement the white text
  • The font isn't appealing at all and there's no difference in font for different elements
  • The header doesn't catch viewers attention from the jump. Although there's a differnce in colors, it should stand out more with maybe size or font style.

Here are the improvements I made:

  • I made the background color a darker red gradient with the black text living in a light blue decortive frame. I aimed to keep the school pride in the design by keeping those red, blue colors but using more calm toned shades.
  • I also added a faded red decorative frame with a subtle brown border to two of the text boxes to create diversity in design.
  • I made the header stand out more while still being visually appealing. I used a bright brown color to complement the dark red and have a "wanted" wild west feel.
  • I used different font for each of the different hierchies, creating seperation and visually pleasing and a fun slide.
  • I added motion to the mascot when a user hovers over it. This was to create a fun interactive element.
